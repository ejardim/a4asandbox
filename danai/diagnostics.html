Runs test:

Runs test checks weather the residuals are randomly distributed. A run is a sequence of the same sign residuals. Few runs indicate a trend or a correlation in the residuals while too many runs may suggest overfitting. The primary output of a runstest is a p-value where:

  • A high p value \((p<0.05)\) suggests that the residuals are randomly distributed
  • A low p value indicates a non-random pattern in the residuals
